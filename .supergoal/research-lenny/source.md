## Buckets (verbatim from article)

### 1. I want to improve my communication skills
> "Friends recommended many different books, and I read them all. But these three had the most practical, impactful, and lasting advice, which is still lodged in my head even now."

- *Nobody Wants to Read Your Sh\*t* — Steven Pressfield
- *On Writing Well* — William Zinsser
- *Storyworthy* — Matthew Dicks

### 2. I want to get better at executing
> "The first two books will give you exactly that, and the third will give you a meta-framework that'll level up how you approach everything at work."

- *The Great CEO Within* — Matt Mochary
- *Scaling People* — Claire Hughes Johnson
- *The Goal* — Eliyahu Goldratt

### 3. I want to get better at strategy
> "I remember reading these books and feeling like, okay, I finally understand what 'strategy' is."

- *Good Strategy/Bad Strategy* — Richard Rumelt
- *Playing to Win* — Roger L. Martin
- *Working Backwards* — Colin Bryar and Bill Carr

### 4. I want to be inspired to build something great
> "This bucket of books might be my favorite of the bunch. They showed me how much a singular (relentless) mind can accomplish."

- *The Making of Prince of Persia* — Jordan Mechner
- *Build* — Tony Fadell
- *Shoe Dog* — Phil Knight

### 5. I want to become a better manager
> "I remember reading High Output Management when I was a baby manager, and it finally taught me what my job actually was."

- *High Output Management* — Andy Grove
- *The Making of a Manager* — Julie Zhuo
- *Radical Candor* — Kim Scott

### 6. I want to become a better leader
> "Push people to do the best work of their lives, but be human about it, and, in the end, the score will take care of itself."

- *Amp It Up* — Frank Slootman
- *The 15 Commitments of Conscious Leadership* — Jim Dethmer, Diana Chapman, Kaley Klemp
- *The Score Takes Care of Itself* — Bill Walsh

### 7. I want to increase my product success rate
> "These classics taught me the fundamentals of product management: talking to customers, prioritizing roadmaps, and what it takes to build something valuable and impactful."

- *The Mom Test* — Rob Fitzpatrick
- *Escaping the Build Trap* — Melissa Perri
- *Continuous Discovery Habits* — Teresa Torres

### 8. I want to level up my product org
> "These books are also some of the most mentioned on my podcast, because they'll shift how you think about the role of product within your organization."

- *Empowered* — Marty Cagan
- *Inspired* — Marty Cagan
- *Thinking in Bets* — Annie Duke

### 9. I want to get better at sales and marketing
> "With AI making it easier to build, distribution is becoming the bigger bottleneck. This is not a natural strength for most builders, which is why you need to develop your marketing and sales muscle."

- *Purple Cow* — Seth Godin
- *Obviously Awesome* — April Dunford
- *Founding Sales* — Peter Kazanjy
